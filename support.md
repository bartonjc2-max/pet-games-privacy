# Support

## Grub - Virtual Pet

Need help with Grub? We're here for you!

### Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I feed my Grub?**
Tap the FEED button to open your inventory. Select a recipe and tap Cook to feed your Grub.

**My Grub is dying! What do I do?**
Feed your Grub quickly to restore hunger, then play games to boost happiness. If health is low, craft healing recipes.

**How do I unlock new recipes?**
Level up by earning XP from feeding, playing, and completing expeditions. Some recipes require finding scroll items while foraging.

**How do I go on expeditions?**
Tap the PACK button to access the Burrow, then select an expedition. Expeditions take time but reward rare loot and XP.

**Can I play offline?**
Yes! Grub works entirely offline. Your progress is saved locally on your device.

**How do ads work?**
Grub is free and ad-supported. You can watch optional rewarded ads for in-game bonuses. We use Google AdMob for advertising.
