# Privacy Policy for Pet Games

**Effective Date:** March 19, 2026

This privacy policy applies to Clank, Grub, and other virtual pet games published by Jesse Barton ("we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how our apps work.

---

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect any personal data directly.** Our games do not require user accounts, login credentials, or any form of registration.

### Game Data

All game progress, settings, and preferences are stored **locally on your device** using standard on-device storage (UserDefaults). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

### Advertising Data

Our apps use **Google AdMob** to display advertisements. AdMob may collect the following types of information:

- Device identifiers (such as Advertising ID)
- Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)
- General device information (device model, OS version)
- IP address (used for approximate location for ad targeting)

For more information on how Google handles data, please review [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### App Tracking Transparency

On iOS, our apps use Apple's **App Tracking Transparency** framework to request your consent before allowing ad personalization tracking. You can change this permission at any time in your device's Settings under Privacy > Tracking.

---

## Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties **except through Google AdMob** as described above. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer any user information.

---

## Children's Privacy

Our games are **not directed at children under the age of 13**. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information through our apps, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

---

## Data Security

Since we do not collect or store personal data on our servers, there is minimal risk of data breach from our side. All game data remains on your device under your control.

---

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
