# Support

## Clank - Virtual Robot Pet

Need help with Clank? We're here for you!

### Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I charge my Clank?**
Tap the CHARGE button to open your inventory. Select a fuel cell and tap Power Up to charge your Clank.

**My Clank is shutting down! What do I do?**
Charge your Clank quickly to restore energy, then play games to boost happiness. If durability is low, craft repair kits.

**How do I unlock new blueprints?**
Level up by earning XP from charging, playing, and completing missions. Some blueprints require finding schematic items while tinkering.

**How do I go on missions?**
Tap the TINKER button to access the Workshop, then select a mission. Missions take time but reward rare parts and XP.

**Can I play offline?**
Yes! Clank works entirely offline. Your progress is saved locally on your device.

**How do ads work?**
Clank is free and ad-supported. You can watch optional rewarded ads for in-game bonuses. We use Google AdMob for advertising.
