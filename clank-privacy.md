# Privacy Policy for Clank

**Effective Date:** March 19, 2026

This privacy policy describes how the iOS app **Clank** ("the App") handles your data. Clank is a virtual pet game published by Jesse Barton.

---

## Overview

Clank is a virtual pet game that does not require any user accounts, login, or personal information to play. Your privacy is important to us, and we have designed the app to minimize data collection.

---

## Information We Collect

### Personal Data

**Clank does not collect any personal data directly.** There are no accounts, no registration, and no forms that request personal information.

### Game Data

All game data -- including your pet's status, progress, and settings -- is stored **locally on your device** using UserDefaults. This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

### Advertising

Clank uses **Google AdMob** to serve advertisements. Through AdMob, the following data may be collected automatically:

- Advertising identifiers (IDFA on iOS)
- Ad interaction data (views, taps)
- Device information (model, operating system version)
- IP address (for approximate geographic targeting)

We use Apple's **App Tracking Transparency** (ATT) framework to request your permission before enabling personalized ad tracking. You may opt out at any time through your device's Settings > Privacy > Tracking.

For details on Google's data practices, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

---

## Data Sharing

No data is shared with third parties except through the AdMob advertising SDK as described above.

---

## Children's Privacy

Clank is **not directed at children under the age of 13**. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

---

## Data Retention

Since all game data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it. Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data.
